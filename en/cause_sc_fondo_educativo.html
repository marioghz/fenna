San Carlos - Education support fund

The FENNA foundation has established our San Carlos - Educational support fund campaign, started in 2021, a period during which it has convened with positive results the solidarity of public and private companies that have wanted to participate in the social development of the country and specifically of the area. North of the country, actively assuming its commitment to the most vulnerable Costa Rican children and adolescents. This project generates part of the resources necessary for the development of the Foundation's programs.

Our purpose is to link all the people and entities that want to join efforts for the benefit of minors who need it so that they allow us to offer a package of school supplies and uniforms for each one, with the aim that they can follow their education correctly and continue attending your educational center.

Characteristics:

  • +75 children and adolescents aided
  • School supplies and uniforms package chosen for each specific case
  • $250.00 average cost for each package
  • Delivery of the benefit on an annual basis

The social group that we have established for this cause consists of low-income children and adolescents at risk from the areas of Pital, Aguas Zarcas, Ciudad Quesada, Monterrey and Buenavista. In addition to that, we continue to add cases from the area to our list, so we require all possible support.

We have carefully chosen the population of minors that is of interest to him and that of those responsible, to remain within a national educational center, with the aim that the supplies provided are of benefit to him and the family that supports him. Each case will be studied and monitored so that this objective is adequately fulfilled.

The cause is based on more than 75 minors in the San Carlos area, who require our collaboration to have an opportunity to grow and study in a dignified way. Helping is very simple, you only need to go to the Donate link and make a donation by credit or debit card, It is also possible to send your donations through mobile SINPE or bank transfer. If you also wish to send us a donation of school supplies please send us a message by clicking on the Contact link and we will be happy to coordinate it.

Our purpose

In the FENNA team we focus our effort on humanitarian support, we believe that there are few actions that change the world like social action with groups at risk, and specifically the population of children and adolescents in Costa Rica. At FENNA, we believe that giving new opportunities to children and adolescents through health food programs and access to educational means allows us to combat inequality and achieve adults with greater future opportunities.

About Us

FENNA is a non-profit organization that is dedicated to promoting educational and nutritional causes for Children and Adolescents in Costa Rica. Find us on our social networks and help us to help.
