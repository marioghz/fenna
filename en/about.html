About us Foundation for Educational Promotion and Nutrition for Children and Adolescents

Our History

FENNA Foundation was established in October 2021, with the aim of promoting social projects for populations at social risk within Costa Rican society. Thus, the FENNA Foundation has focused its efforts on supporting social causes that seek the well-being of children and adolescents with special nutrition and education needs. The families of children and adolescents involved in our causes can be benefited since FENNA offers comprehensive support projects for the well-being of all.

Although our work is specified in two lines of action, our goal is more ambitious. We want permanent and profound changes that allow us to provide better opportunities to future adults of the Costa Rican people.

Mission

Promote and support social causes for populations with social risk on education and nutrition issues for all of Costa Rica.

Vision

Develop causes that satisfy basic Costa Rican needs.

Values

  • Morality
  • Respect
  • Altruism
  • Colaboration

Our reason for being

At FENNA we are committed to giving our best, this is reflected in each campaign, each launch and each content that is delivered to our donors and collaborators. Everyone at FENNA is an example of what service, passion and social commitment mean.

Part of our approach is to do well what each of us has to do. We are clear about the importance and responsibility that our work involves.

In the FENNA team we focus our energies on humanitarian support, we believe that there are few actions that change the world such as social action with groups at risk, and specifically the population of children and adolescents in Costa Rica. At FENNA we believe that giving new opportunities to children and adolescents through health food programs and access to educational means allows us to combat inequality and achieve adults with greater opportunities for the future.
